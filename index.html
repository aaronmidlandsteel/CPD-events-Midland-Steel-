About Midland Steel

Innovation • Quality • Engineering Excellence

Midland Steel is the leading steel reinforcement company specialising in innovative rebar solutions and advanced manufacturing techniques for the construction industry.

Committed to precision, quality and sustainability, the company delivers cutting-edge offsite manufactured reinforcement products that improve efficiency, reduce installation time and support safer construction practices.

Over the past 15 years, Midland Steel has pioneered the use of Building Information Modelling (BIM), digital design and offsite manufacturing to develop innovative reinforcement solutions for complex construction projects.

This expertise has enabled the company to create smarter, more efficient and cost-effective methods for the design, manufacture and installation of reinforced concrete structures.
